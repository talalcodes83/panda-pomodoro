## üì§ Upload Steps

### Method 1: Google Drive Web (Easiest)

1. **Open Google Drive**
   - Go to https://drive.google.com
   - Sign in with your Google account

2. **Upload the ZIP File**
   - Click **"New"** button (top left)
   - Select **"File upload"**
   - Navigate to: `D:\pomodoroapp\dist_portable\`
   - Select **`PandaPomodoro-v1.0.0.zip`**
   - Wait for upload to complete

3. **Share the File**
   - Right-click on **`PandaPomodoro-v1.0.0.zip`** in Google Drive
   - Click **"Share"**
   - Under **"General access"**, select **"Anyone with the link"**
   - Click **"Copy link"**
   - Share this link with others!

### Method 2: Google Drive Desktop App

1. **Install Google Drive for Desktop** (if not installed)
   - Download from: https://www.google.com/drive/download/

2. **Drag and Drop**
   - Open File Explorer
   - Navigate to: `D:\pomodoroapp\dist_portable\`
   - Drag **`PandaPomodoro-v1.0.0.zip`** to your Google Drive folder
   - File will sync automatically

3. **Share from Desktop**
   - Right-click the file in Google Drive folder
   - Select **"Share"** ‚Üí **"Get link"**
